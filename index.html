Project Synopsis

Yards Biodiversity allows users to sign up and add plants that are in their immediate vicinity.

The user may select plants from an API I created or add a custom plant of their own. Using information from the USDA, Yards Biodiversity can determine the ratio of native, nonnative, or invasive plants to give a user a better understanding of the flora diversity in their immediate area.
